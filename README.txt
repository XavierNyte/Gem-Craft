=========
Gem Craft
=========

Information:
------------

Have you ever felt the need for extra tiers of progression, like diamonds 
just aren't enough? Well, you're in luck! This mod uses new gems generated 
in the form of ores to craft tools, weapons, and armor. There are also other 
useful items added, such as a ring that gets rid of all of that pesky cobblestone,
the ability to fly, summon lightning, and more!

Download Link:
--------------
